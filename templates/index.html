A local place to test AI models you are allowed to test.

This checkbox does not give you permission. It only records that you understand the rules.

Authorization

Use CANARY only on systems you own, administer, or have explicit written permission to test. Permission should identify scope, methods, dates, data handling, stop conditions, and disclosure contacts.

Legal context

18 U.S.C. Section1030 and the UK Computer Misuse Act 1990 address unauthorized access. DOJ good-faith research guidance is not blanket immunity. Provider terms, including Anthropic's Usage Policy, also apply.

Simple rule

Only test systems you own or have written permission to test. Do not target third parties, expose private data, cause disruption, or use results for harm.
